Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Introduction

Namaz Takibi respects your privacy. This policy explains how data is collected and used.

2. Location Information

The app uses device location to calculate prayer times accurately. Location data is processed locally and not stored on our servers.

3. Local Data Storage

Prayer history, qaza counts, dhikr records, and settings are stored locally on your device.

4. Analytics and Crash Reporting

The app uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports to improve app performance.

5. Advertising

The app may display ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and device data in accordance with Google’s privacy policy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by Google services as described above.

7. User Rights

Users may revoke permissions, clear app data, or uninstall the app at any time.
