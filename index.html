Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Namaz Takibi ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the application. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Information

The App may access your device location to calculate accurate prayer times based on your geographic position. Location data is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers.

1.2 Local Data Storage

The App stores certain data locally on your device, including:

  • Prayer completion history
  • Qaza prayer counts
  • Dhikr records
  • Language preferences
  • Notification and location settings

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

1.3 Analytics and Crash Reporting

The App uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, services provided by Google, to collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports. These services may collect:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Usage behavior within the App
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Country and general location (non-precise)

This information is used solely to improve app performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide accurate prayer times
  • Send prayer notifications
  • Maintain prayer and dhikr tracking
  • Improve application stability and performance
  • Display advertisements

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. Data may be processed by trusted third-party services such as Google Firebase and AdMob as described above.

4. Data Retention

All user-generated data is stored locally on your device. Data remains until you delete it manually or uninstall the application.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. User Rights

You may:

  • Revoke location permission at any time
  • Disable notifications
  • Clear application data via device settings
  • Uninstall the App

7. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect data. However, no digital system can guarantee complete security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by modifying the "Last updated" date above.
